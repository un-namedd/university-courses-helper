## UoG Degree Planner ({{uniUrl}})

**{{uniServiceFullName}}** is a free degree-planning tool for University of Guelph programs. You can use it without an account (plans stay in your browser). If you sign in, we store your plans in the cloud.

| Category | Examples | How we get it | Why |
|----------|----------|---------------|-----|
| Identifiers | Email address, account user ID | You sign in with Google (email scope only) or email magic link | Account and cloud sync |
| User content | Saved degree plans (program choice, terms, placed courses, Area of Emphasis) | You build plans in the app | Cloud save and restore |
| Share links | A random share ID if you make a plan public | You enable sharing | Read-only link for others |
| Technical data | IP address, browser/device type, server logs | Automatic when you use the Service | Security, debugging, abuse prevention |
| Cookies & local storage | Supabase session cookies, planner state, theme | Your browser | Login, offline plans, preferences |

**Course catalog data** (program requirements, course descriptions) is static public JSON served from our CDN. It is **not** personal information.

We do **not** receive your Google password. Google sign-in is limited to your **email address** for account identification. See [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for how Google handles your Google account.

We do **not** sell your personal information or use planner data for advertising.

### How we use planner information

- Create and secure your account when you choose cloud sync.
- Save, load, and delete your degree plans.
- Generate read-only share links when you explicitly make a plan public.
- Operate, secure, and improve the planner.

## How long we keep data

- **Account data** — while your account is active and for a short period after you ask us to delete it (we aim to complete deletion requests within about 30 days, subject to backups and legal holds).

- **Cookie consent record** — stored in your browser until you clear it.
